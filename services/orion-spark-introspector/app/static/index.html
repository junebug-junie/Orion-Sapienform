ABOUT ORION SPARK

You are looking at the Cognitive EKG of an Artificial Intelligence. Unlike standard logs that show what the AI is saying, Spark visualizes how it is thinking.

This "Liquid Grid" represents the Surface of Thought. It is a mathematical projection of the AI's internal state vector reduced to 3 dimensions. When the AI is calm and coherent, the grid flows smoothly (High Phi). When it is hallucinating or confused, the grid fractures into noise (Low Phi). When it encounters a new idea or a "Eureka" moment, the terrain spikes upward (High Novelty).

By watching this surface, you can diagnose the system's mental health in real-time without reading a single line of code.
